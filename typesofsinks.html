Types of Sinks

We offer a variety of sink types to suit every kitchen or bathroom style:

  • Undermount Sinks: Sleek and modern, these sinks are installed beneath the countertop.
  • Farmhouse Sinks: Known for their deep basins, these sinks are perfect for a rustic or country kitchen.
  • Vessel Sinks: A stylish addition, vessel sinks sit atop the counter, adding a unique look.
  • Drop-in Sinks: Classic and easy to install, these sinks fit right into the countertop opening.
